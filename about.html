About

A Polish music band playing Christian music. Founded in 2015 by members of the band who recorded the song ‘Blessed are the Merciful’, which became the anthem of the World Youth Day 2016. On May 6, 2016, the band was registered in the National Court Register as a limited liability company. The band participates in many events promoting the Christian faith, such as the Festival of David’s Psalms, pilgrimages to Marian shrines, and initiatives for young people. In 2014, the band members recorded the song ‘Blessed are the Merciful’, which became the anthem of the World Youth Day 2016 in Krakow. The song has been translated into over 15 foreign languages. In September 2022, the band niemaGOtu, together with Caritas Archdiocese of Katowice, started working on a music video for the song ‘Come’, which is intended to help raise money for refugees from Ukraine.
